ICOPE

ICOPE - Integrated Care for Older People - it is a guideline document developed and structured by the World Health Organization for the diagnosis, treatment and healthcare for older people. The tests conducted by our hospital strictly follows this guideline and that is how we ensure the authenticity and the quality of the diagnosis that we do in the hospital. For more information about ICOPE do visit the website of the World Health Organization
